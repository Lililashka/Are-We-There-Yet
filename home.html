ARE WE THERE YET

FESTIVAL OF CREATIVITY

May 31, June 1-2, 2020


ARE-WE-THERE-YET is a micro-festival showcasing lightning talks on the future of creativity.
We are creating a live community of wonder to build a record of the next generation of design, arts, and crafts.

Pre-registration will be open soon.
Drop us an email to get notified first.





?!
How?

Inspiration, awe, and fun will be served directly to your living room. All you need to do is get your ticket in advance, prepare your favorite snack, and come join us from your couch.
~

Who?

The first chapter of ARE-WE-THERE-YET includes a thoughtful speaker line-up that will challenge your thinking on topics such as design for the decentralized web, immersive experiences, crypto art, and beyond. We want to keep you exploring and experimenting, even in the darkest of times.
~

Ticket price

All tickets are available for free! However, you need to get them in advance in order to guarantee the spot in a conference as well as the invitation to our community chat and resources.
~

Team

We are a group of designers and artists who want to make sure that creativity and great ideas are contagious.
~
